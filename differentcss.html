A NOTE ABOUT KERALA

KERALA[A GODS OWN COUNTRY]

There are a total of 14 districts in Kerala, which are generally grouped into three parts based on geographical, historical, and cultural similarities. The Northern Kerala districts are Kasaragod, Kannur, Wayanad, Kozhikode, and Malappuram. The Central Kerala districts are Palakkad, Thrissur, and Ernakulam. The Southern Kerala districts are Idukki, Kottayam, Alappuzha, Pathanamthitta, Kollam, and Thiruvananthapuram. Malappuram is the most populated district, while Wayanad is the least populated. Palakkad is the largest district by area, while Alappuzha is the smallest. The districts are further divided into taluks, Municipal Corporations, Municipalities, and Gram panchayats.
